4a Following a description: diagrams, maps and plans
Tips for map labelling

Listen carefully for the starting point – you need to start at the correct place in order to get the questions right.

OK, we're at the south entrance to the mall now. I'll tell you where some of the main shops are and then you can go off and explore on your own. Can we meet up though at the pizza restaurant at half past twelve? You'll find that about halfway along this street in front of us now. If you come to the fountain, that means you've gone too far. It's just before that on the left-hand side.

Explanation: It’s very important to locate yourself in the right place. For example, if you start at the west rather than the south entrance then you will think the pizza restaurant is at A rather than B.
